The Curriculum

10 Modules. One Transformative Journey.

Each module combines knowledge with hands-on practice. Together, they form a complete immersion in the world of poetry.

MODULE 01
Analysing Classical & Modern Poetry
The Art of Deep Reading
Sharpen critical thinking through close reading of verse across eras — learning to unravel metaphor, rhythm, and layered meaning.
MODULE 02
Reading Multilingual Poetry
Voices Across Languages
Expand cultural empathy by experiencing verse in Bengali, Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, English, and more.
MODULE 03
Poetry Debates & Discussions
The Kavya Sabha Tradition
Build eloquence and confidence through structured poetry debates and spontaneous commentary.
MODULE 04
Learning About Poets & Their Lives
The Human Behind the Verse
Discover how biography, exile, grief, and longing shaped the world's greatest poets.
MODULE 05
History of Poems & Poets
Poetry as Civilisational Memory
Journey from the Rig Veda through Persian ghazals, Bengali renaissance poetry, to spoken word.
MODULE 06
Writing Original Poetry
Finding Your Voice
Unlock self-expression through guided writing in multiple forms — haiku, ghazal, free verse.
MODULE 07
Encapsulating History in Verse
The Poet as Witness
Explore how poetry preserves emotional truths that textbooks and data cannot capture.
MODULE 08
Poetry as Mindful Practice
Presence Through Language
Cultivate presence and deep listening through the meditative dimensions of poetry.
MODULE 09
Bridging Cultures & Generations
Poetry as Living Connection
Experience poetry as a bridge connecting people, cultures, languages, and eras.
MODULE 10
Fuelling Innovation & Creative Thinking
Poetry as a Cognitive Superpower
Discover how poetic thinking drives lateral innovation in any field.
